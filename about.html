Get to know me!

Hi, I'm Tiereney, an Instructional Designer dedicated to creating engaging and impactful learning experiences. Currently, I am studying Instructional Design and Technology at the University of Memphis. My journey in instructional design started with a strong interest in how people learn and how to make learning engaging, effective, and accessible for all types of learners. What excites me most about instructional design is the ability to creatively solve problems and make a positive impact on the learning process. I'm particularly interested in how technology can be used to enhance learning, and I am eager to continue learning about the latest trends and tools in the field. Looking ahead, I hope to gain more hands-on experience and I'm excited to apply the skills I've developed in real-world scenarios. I am continuously working on expanding my knowledge and becoming a well-rounded instructional designer. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see the projects I've worked on during my studies, and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to connect!
