E-Learning Chatbot

est read time: 8 mins

Overview

Understanding AI and manipulating it for a learner's benefit can achieve impressive results! This manipulation of AI is in the form of a chatbot for learners to use. This provides a solution for student's to receive readily available learning support for college coursework.

Role


• User Researcher
• Instructional Designer

Duration


• Ongoing

Skills


• Needs Assessment
• Instructional Alignment

Tools

The Problem

How can we improve the support for students taking online college courses?

Proposed Solution

A readily available AI Chatbot curatored to learners' course content and learning styles.

Design Process

Analysis Phase

Target Audience

This design intervention's primary target audience is college students who take online classes. The primary audience will have a basic background in using a computer and have basic knowledge of interacting with AI related to course content. Students will complete their learning at various times and days due to the flexibility of online learning. The students have control over the pacing they want to consume course content over the week.
