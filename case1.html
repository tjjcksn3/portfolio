Overview

Understanding AI and manipulating it for a learner's benefit can achieve impressive results! This manipulation of AI is in the form of a chatbot for learners to use. This can help students gain knowledge in subjects they must learn with precise support in all subject areas.

The Problem

Many students encounter challenges accessing timely assistance for online coursework. This can negatively affect their learning outcomes, academic performance, and reduce knowledge construction. This lack of readily available support resources can lead learners to experience increased frustration and disengagement. This case study aims to explore an innovated solution that will enhance accessibility to academic support, to create a more effective learning environment.
